I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Chair for Decentralized Information Systems and Data Management at the Technical University of Munich, under the supervision of the amazing Prof. Viktor Leis.
Before joining TUM, I received a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Vietnam National University and an M.Sc. in Computer Science at Sungkyunkwan University, South Korea.

My main research area is Database Management Systems (DBMSs), focusing on core research topics such as concurrency control, buffer management, and storage.
