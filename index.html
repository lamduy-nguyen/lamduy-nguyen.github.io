Boltzmannstr. 3

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Chair for Decentralized Information Systems and Data Management at Technical University of Munich, under the supervision of the amazing Prof. Viktor Leis. My main research area is Database Management Systems (DBMSs), focusing on core research topics such as concurrency control, buffer management, and storage.

Before joining TUM, I received a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Vietnam National University - University of Engineering and Technology in 2017. After spending a few years in the industry, I returned to academia and completed an M.Sc. in Computer Science at Sungkyunkwan University, South Korea.
